Play School Management System – Proposal

This proposal outlines a budget-friendly Play School Management System designed to streamline daily
operations and improve parent communication, with a strong focus on transparency and simplicity.
Key Features
• Student Admission & Profile Management
• Class & Section Management
• Daily Student Attendance
• Parent Communication – Daily Communication Report (custom format)
• Basic Fee Management & Receipts
• Minimal Staff Management (Teachers & Helpers)
• Essential Reports (Attendance, Fees, Daily Report)
Daily Parent Communication Report
A custom daily report shared with parents including routine activities, mood tracking, food intake, sleep
duration, toileting notes, teacher remarks, and daily learning summary. Reports can be shared as PDF via
WhatsApp or viewed through a simple parent login.
What Is Not Included
• CRM or enquiry management
• Marketing or lead tracking
• Payroll & accounting
• Online payment gateway
• Advanced analytics or native mobile app
Deployment & Access
• Web-based system (mobile-friendly)
• Role-based access for Admin, Teachers, and Parents
• Secure access with parent visibility limited to their own child
This solution is ideal for small to mid-sized play schools looking for an affordable system that builds parent
trust through structured daily updates and organized records.
